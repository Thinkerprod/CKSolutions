About Us

Shauna Hill and Cory Kutschker combined their individual talents to create a family business supporting other local businesses. Their focus is on providing all the administrative tools needed so small business owners can do what they do best, selling their products and services. Both feel compelled to serve their community with integrity and have spent extensive time helping out their favourite charities.

About Shauna Hill

Shauna has over 35 years experience assisting small business with their accounting needs. A long-term business owner with 'been there, done that' experience is often a welcome addition to your team arsenal. And everyone deserves a winning team.

  • Certified post-secondary business instructor: taught business administration at the college level and facilitated numerous business start-up workshops
  • Successful business plan and proposal writer, including grant writing for non-profit groups
  • Has held numerous leadership positions as a non-profit board member and Toastmaster

About Cory Kutschker

Cory is a certified web developer with an eye for detail. He has a wide field of experience, ranging from public-speaking to video editing. He has a passion for writing and excels in editing. He is a natural problem solver and often looks for creative solutions.

  • Assisted in developing an app for Heritage Insurance, Moose Jaw
  • Has a diploma in Business Information Systems
  • Has a Bachelor of Arts in Theology from Briercrest College
